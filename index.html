Guess the country

The objective is to guess the country by its flag.
For more information, click the "How to play" button.

The objective of the game is pretty simple: you're shown a country flag and you have 10 seconds to guess which the country is. You're free to choose from two modes:

  • Normal mode:
    • You have 10 seconds to guess
    • Each correct answers gives you 10 points + the seconds left multiplied by 10.
  • Endless mode:
    • You have 10 seconds to guess
    • You're allowed 3 incorrect guesses
    • For each answers in a row, you get your multiplier increased by 1.
    • Each correct answer gives you 10 points + the seconds left multiplied by the current multplier value.
